Supharada Jundok

Hi! I'm Supharada Jundok (ศุภรดา จันทร์ดก), but you can call me Keira J or 李沛妤.

I'm currently an M.S. student in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Taiwan. I recently graduated with a B.S. in CSIE from National Dong Hwa University, and along the way I found myself really drawn to machine learning and research.

These days, I'm especially interested in machine learning, computer vision, UAV remote sensing, and AI safety. I enjoy building things that actually work in practice, not just on paper, and I'm always curious about how research ideas can be turned into something useful in the real world. In the future, I hope to work as a Machine Learning Scientist or Machine Learning Engineer, while continuing to explore how to make AI more trustworthy.

Outside of research, I like writing, learning languages, and exploring life in Taiwan. When I'm not in front of my laptop, you'll probably find me training Muay Thai, Kickboxing, Boxing, or Weight Training.

Right now, I'm based between Tainan, Taiwan, and Khon Kaen, Thailand.
